About Us

VISION

To develop ICT's entrepreneurial think-tank, where students develop business acumen and transform into industry-ready professionals

MISSION

Bridging the gap between theory and practical entrepreneurship

  • Educate - Conducting workshops and seminars on core business domains such as finance, and strategic decision-making
  • Engage - Organizing competitions and events which model real-life business challenges
  • Empower - Providing a forum to discuss issues that involve global markets, policies, and economic disruptions
  • Execute - Equipping students with managerial skills related to supply chains, marketing strategies, corporate finance, and investment decisions
